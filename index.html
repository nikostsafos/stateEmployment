Data on employment from Bureau of Labor Statistics, State and Area Employment, Hours and Earnings (March 3, 2017); data on labor force participation from Bureau of Labor Statistics, Local Area Unemployment Statistics (March 24, 2017). Employment data are shown as a percent of total non-farm employment. Labor force and total employment data are as a percent of total civilian noninstitutional population (aged 16 and over). A detailed breakdown of the subsectors under each sector can be found here. For more: https://github.com/nikostsafos/stateEmployment.
